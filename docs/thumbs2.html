GroundForge

A thumbnail index for matching sets of bobbin lace diagrams.

Hover with your mouse over a thumbnail (or tap) for a tooltip with links for working diagrams and pricking prototypes.

These patterns are collected in 2012/2014 from TesseLace.com and are just a tip of the iceberg of possible patterns.

You can reorder the thumbnails according to your own classification by dragging them around. The next time you visit the page, the thumbnails will have the original order. So save the changed page on your hard disk and next time open the saved page. The first group of thumbnails link to patterns with several patches on the pattern sheet. These patches vary in shape or are mirrored versions. The second group has just a single patch per pattern.
