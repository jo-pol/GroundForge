GroundForge

Choose patterns and stitches to generate diagrams for grounds of bobbin lace.

Thread diagrams of a plain Torchon ground (with respectively ct or ctct), are pair diagrams for a Paris or Flanders alias five-hole ground. The method can be applied to many more stitches, combination of stitches and even repeated. This results in a myriad of possibilities. The generated diagrams can help to select the interesting ones with an educated guess.

Get started

Option A:
Initialize the parameters with one of the 376 patterns listed with thumbnails.
Option B:
As an alternative decide how many stitches you want to choose at the first step for one of the base patterns and vary stitches at step two.
Advanced options:
Read the user guide to vary on traditional patterns.
Warning:
With complex stitches and large numbers of rows and columns, your browser can get overwhelmed with step two or three.
