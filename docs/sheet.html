GroundForge

Pricking prototypes for bobbin lace grounds.

Patterns on the thumbnail page have two links: to thread/pair diagrams and to corresponding pricking prototypes on this page. With the diagrams you can vary stitches, download and edit a pricking prototype to nudge pin positions and turn it into a pricking in the desired style. Most important clue about reshaping patterns from the user guide: dots with the same color should match when "sewing" repeats together. These dots should also move together, a process made easy by state of the art general purpose drawing editors.
